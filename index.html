Alex Barth - Marine Community Ecology

Alex Barth is a Ph.D. student at University of South Carolina in Dr. Joshua Stone's zooplankton ecology lab. His research focuses on studying zooplankton community structure and dynamics across a variety of ecosystems.

Specific research interests include:

  • The role of protists in zooplankton communities
  • Successional patterns across plankton groups
  • Imaging technology for plankton
  • Utilizing community science to improve plankton monitoring
