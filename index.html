Hello!

I just finished my Ph.D. at University of South CarolinaDr. Josh Stone's Zooplankton Ecology Lab. Soon I will be starting a post-doc with Dr. Jordan Casey and Dr. Mevin Hooten

My research centers on describing plankton community structure and how it varies across a variety of scales. My past work has focused on describing population structure and behavior in open-ocean systems. Now, much of my work focuses on estuarine plankton systems. These respond drastically to climatic and anthropogenic changes. My work is focused on describing their variation and documenting food-web shifts from a bottom-up perspective. To study plankton, I use a variety of new tools and am always interested in improving methodology.

Outside of my research, I have a lot of fun with R programming language. I'm always excited to chat about code!

Take a look at my current projects to see what I'm working on.
